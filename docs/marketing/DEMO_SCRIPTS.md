# BookBounty Demo Scripts: The Transition Scenarios

## 1. The Student Purge (60 Seconds)
**Goal:** Speed and Cash.
- **Narrator:** "It's 2 PM on graduation Saturday. The moving truck is here in two hours. I've got 50 textbooks and I'm not hauling them back to my parents' house. I'm setting my goal to **'Financial Optimizer.'**"
- **Visual:** Rapid-fire scanning of 5 textbooks.
- **Narrator:** "Scan one: 2023 Physics. **SELL ($120)**. Scan two: 2015 Econ. **DISCARD**. Scan three: Calculus 101. **SELL ($45)**. In 60 seconds, I've identified $165 in gas money and cleared three feet of shelf space. No research, just scan and pack."

## 2. The Retiree Legacy (90 Seconds)
**Goal:** Meaningful Preservation.
- **Narrator:** "Martha is moving from a 40-year family home. Every book is a memory, but the condo is small. She's set her goal to **'Legacy Downsize.'**"
- **Visual:** Martha scanning a worn hardcover book on local history.
- **Narrator:** "She scans this obscure regional guide. The AI doesn't just check the price; it recognizes the rarity. Result: **'Offer for Acquisition.'** The AI informs her: 'A local historical society is seeking this title.' Martha isn't throwing away a memory; she's contributing to an archive. Decision fatigue is gone; purpose is back."

## 3. The Institutional Scout (90 Seconds)
**Goal:** B2B Intelligence and Acquisition.
- **Narrator:** "I'm the Acquisitions Librarian at State University. Our budget is tight, and we have major gaps in our regional 20th-century poetry collection. I've indexed our **Collection Development Policy** into BookBounty."
- **Visual:** Dashboard view showing an 'Acquisition Match' notification.
- **Narrator:** "Look at this alert. A user 30 miles away just scanned a self-published chapbook that matches our criteria. I hit 'Request for Acquisition.' The system generates a shipping label for the owner. We get the book we've been hunting for years; they get a premium and a donation receipt. We just sourced a rare item without a dealer markup."

## 4. The "Magic Loop" Summary (30 Seconds)
**Goal:** Technical Impression.
- **Narrator:** "Scan. Gemini 2.5 Flash reasons through the metadata. Market data is pulled. The decision is made. Whether you're a student needing cash, a retiree needing space, or a library needing gems—BookBounty is the intelligence layer for the private library."
