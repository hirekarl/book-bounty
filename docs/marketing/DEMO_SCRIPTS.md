# BookBounty Demo Scripts

## 1. 3-Minute Video Demo Script: "The Future of Your Shelf"

- **[0:00-0:30] The Pain Point**
  - **Visual:** A person staring at an overflowing bookshelf with "For Sale" signs in the background.
  - **Narrator:** "Books are heavy—not just on the shelf, but on the mind. When it's time to move or downsize, how do you decide what stays and what goes? Most people spend hours on Google and eBay, only to give up and donate everything for nothing."

- **[0:30-1:00] Setting the Intent**
  - **Visual:** UI shot of the Dashboard. User selects "The Financial Optimizer" goal.
  - **Narrator:** "With BookBounty, you start with a mission. I'm choosing the 'Financial Optimizer' goal. My priority? Maximize cash for my move. The AI now acts as my professional appraiser."

- **[1:00-2:00] The "Magic" Loop**
  - **Visual:** Split screen. Left: User scanning a book. Right: Instant UI update with Book Cover, AI Recommendation [SELL], and Reasoning.
  - **Narrator:** "I scan this 1990s technical manual. Instant result: **DISCARD**. Reasoning: 'Outdated content, zero market demand.' Next, I scan a hardcover classic. AI suggests **SELL** at $35. It pulls live eBay data and even drafts the marketplace description for me. No research required."

- **[2:00-2:45] The Vision: Institutional Matching**
  - **Visual:** UI shows a specialized badge: "Institutional Match Found."
  - **Narrator:** "But here's the game-changer. I scan an obscure regional history book. The AI flags it for **Offer for Acquisition**. A local university library is looking for this specific title. One click, and I've helped an archive while earning a premium. This is the future of the circular book economy."

- **[2:45-3:00] Conclusion**
  - **Visual:** The person from the start, now with a neat, curated shelf and a "Shelf Impact" dashboard showing $400 earned.
  - **Narrator:** "BookBounty. Your personal AI librarian. Downsize with intelligence."
