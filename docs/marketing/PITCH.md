# BookBounty: The AI Librarian for Life's Transitions

## The Problem: "The Burden of the Shelf"
Physical books are more than paper; they are heavy anchors. Whether it’s a **college student** clearing a dorm room in 48 hours or a **retiree** downsizing 40 years of memories, the manual effort to value and re-home a collection is overwhelming.

Traditional options fail:
- **Goodreads:** A passive, "read-only" catalog.
- **Ziffit/ThriftBooks:** Low-value buyback traps that ignore rare or scholarly value.
- **Donation Bins:** A "blind drop" that often leads to books being pulped.

## The Solution: Your Personal AI Librarian
BookBounty is an AI-driven **downsizing consultant** that turns decision fatigue into actionable clarity. By combining real-time market data with the bibliographic intelligence of **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, BookBounty provides goal-aligned recommendations (KEEP, SELL, DONATE, DISCARD).

## Core Innovation: Dual-Cohort Intelligence
Unlike generic scanners, BookBounty's AI is anchored by the user’s specific life transition:
- **The College Student Purge:** Optimized for speed and liquidity. "What can I sell for gas money today?"
- **The Retiree Downsize:** Optimized for legacy and preservation. "Where will this book be most valued?"

## The Future: The Institutional Scout (V3)
We are building the connective tissue between private collections and academic institutions. Our V3 vision introduces **"Offer for Acquisition"** — an intelligence layer where libraries and archives can discover and acquire rare or specialty titles directly from the owner’s shelf. 

BookBounty doesn't just clear a room; it **surfaces hidden gems** for the next generation of scholars.

## Technical Edge
- **Intelligence:** Gemini 2.5 Flash via `instructor` for sub-second structured bibliographic reasoning.
- **Accuracy:** Live eBay API integration for high-fidelity market valuation.
- **Scale:** A software-only, "zero-warehouse" model that makes the private library liquid.

**BookBounty. Your personal AI librarian. Downsize with intelligence.**
