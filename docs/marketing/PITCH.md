# BookBounty: The AI Librarian for Your Personal Collection

## The Problem: "The Heavy Shelf"
Physical books are more than paper; they are memories, status symbols, and burdens. Most personal collections are static, cluttered, and overwhelming. When life shifts—a move, a new baby, a career change—the manual effort required to research, value, and re-home a collection is paralyzing. Existing apps are either passive catalogs (Goodreads) or low-value buyback traps (Ziffit).

## The Solution: Your Personal AI Librarian
BookBounty is an AI-driven **downsizing consultant** that turns decision fatigue into actionable clarity. By combining real-time **eBay market valuation** with the sophisticated reasoning of **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, BookBounty provides instant, goal-aligned recommendations (KEEP, SELL, DONATE, DISCARD).

## The Core Innovation: Goal-Aware Triage
Unlike generic scanners, BookBounty's AI is anchored by your **Culling Goal**. Whether you're a "Financial Optimizer" looking for cash or a "Minimalist" seeking space, the AI interprets metadata and condition through the lens of your specific life transition.

## The Future: The Institutional Marketplace
We are evolving into the connective tissue between private owners and institutions. Our V3 vision introduces **"Offer for Acquisition,"** a software-first marketplace where libraries and archives can discover and acquire rare or specialty titles directly from users, bypassing traditional, high-friction supply chains.

## Technical Edge
- **Intelligence:** Gemini 2.5 Flash via `instructor` for sub-second structured reasoning.
- **Accuracy:** Live eBay API integration for high-fidelity market pricing.
- **Scale:** Multi-tenant React/Django stack designed for global availability and B2B white-labeling.
