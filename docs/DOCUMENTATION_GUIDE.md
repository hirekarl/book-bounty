# BookBounty Documentation Guide

Welcome to the BookBounty library. This project is more than just a tool; it is a journey from personal downsizing to institutional matchmaking. To help you navigate our stacks, we have organized our documentation into three distinct paths.

---

## 🧭 The User's Path: Taming the Triage
*For those who have too many books and not enough time.*

If you are here to solve a real-world problem—clearing a room, moving house, or honoring a loved one's estate—this documentation is for you. We focus on the *why* and the *how* of the culling process.

1.  **[Project README](../README.md)**: The front door. Start here for the high-level overview, quick-start installation, and the "What It Does" summary.
2.  **[Cohort Narratives](marketing/COHORT_NARRATIVES.md)**: Find your story. Whether you are a student moving on or a retiree letting go, see how BookBounty adapts to your specific life transition.
3.  **[Demo Scripts](marketing/DEMO_SCRIPTS.md)**: See the workflow in action. These scripts describe the seamless transition from scan to recommendation.
4.  **[Usage Guide](../README.md#usage-guide)**: A step-by-step walkthrough of setting goals, scanning ISBNs, and resolving your collection.

---

## 🎓 The Peer's Path: Fellowship & Architecture
*For developers, designers, and students of the craft.*

This project was built within a fellowship framework, prioritizing transparency, iterative design, and AI-first engineering. If you are here to understand how we built the "brain" or how the agentic staff collaborates, dive in here.

1.  **[The Vision](architecture/VISION.md)**: The foundational logic of the AI-driven triage engine (v2).
2.  **[AI Specification](architecture/AI_SPEC.md)**: A deep dive into the Gemini 2.5 Flash integration, the `instructor` library, and the confidence-scoring engine.
3.  **[Orchestration Log](staff/REFLECTION.md)**: The "black box" of the project. Follow the session-by-session reflections of the agents (Atlas, Forge, Prism, etc.) as they build the system.
4.  **[Fellowship Reflection](fellowship/reflections/workflow_automation.md)**: A personal essay on meta-automation and the philosophical shift from manual sorting to agent orchestration.
5.  **[Build History](roadmap/completed/)**: A chronological record of the 10 phases that brought BookBounty from a concept to a multi-tenant platform.

## 📈 The Stakeholder's Path: Strategic Future

BookBounty's MVP is a personal utility; its future is a marketplace. If you are here to evaluate the long-term viability and B2B potential of the platform, these documents outline our strategic roadmap.

5.  **[Deployment & Scalability](architecture/DEPLOYMENT.md)**: How we host on Render and our path to a multi-tenant production environment.
